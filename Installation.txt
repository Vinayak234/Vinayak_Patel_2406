Virtualwaer is revolutionize way of shopping. 
Forget going to the store and waiting in long lines to cash out or wasting time trying on various t-shirts or jeans just to realize there too tight or they are too loose. 
With our product from Virtualwear, we are going to make shopping more efficient and people will save time and some hair pulling.
Our product specified to the user’s measurements which would be taken by attaching an automated measuring tape to the various required points like the waist or the chest to get an accurate measurement of the user.
There are multiple points that will be measured throughout the body to generate a virtual person with their exact measurements. 
The user can then choose any store they want to shop at and choose to put together an outfit.
